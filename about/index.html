Core Devs

BurstDev
What's there to say, he's the man when it comes to Burst! He developed HDD mining (Proof-of-Capacity) and created Burstcoin in August, 2014. Still central in all BURST development.
Bitcointalk user

burstdev @ #burst-coin & #burstcoin on http://webchat.freenode.net/

vbcs
Brilliant coder who worked with other coins before, such as Qora. Special expertise is CIYAM's Automated Transactions (AT), and also associated CIYAM Developer. Now concentrating on Atomic Cross-Chain Transfers (ACCTs).
Bitcointalk user

At times: vbcs @ #burst-coin & #burstcoin on http://webchat.freenode.net/

HALL of FAME - Economic Supporters


Let us tell you some of the conditions under which BURST is developed and why we have this last heading.

We didn't do any premine, we didn't create promises to get investors into an uncertain IPO to collect huge amounts of BTC for future research.

BURST chose a different way: We started without any funding and got investors by showing results - instead of spreading promises that might, or might not, be fulfilled.

Since August, 2014, volunteers have developed the coin: Please, have a look at our section Angels of BURST below. As you see under that heading, BURST lives on donations and generous Devs who use their own money to improve BURST.


Do YOU want to support BURST and be listed as an Angel?

Your donations, how small they might be, will push BURST forward! The gratitude we who work with BURST on a daily basis are immense and makes us work even harder.
